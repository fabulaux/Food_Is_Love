Bombo

Featured Vendor

Bombo is a casual, broth-based seafood concept steeped in bold flavor and fresh, seasonal ingredients. Created by Chef Mark Peel, one of the fathers of California cuisine, Bombo also features a modern day fish market curated by Chef Peel. Chef Mark Peel’s desire to contribute to the renaissance of downtown Los Angeles was a significant factor in choosing the Grand Central Market to launch his new restaurant.

 

Sourcing seasonal, sustainable seafood, the Bombo Fresh Seafood Market offers different varieties of clams, mussels, shrimp; squid; tuna, including yellowtail; salmon; rock cod and other local, mild-flavored white fish; black cod; and oysters by the dozen. Many of these items are featured in the dishes on Bombo’s menu. Chef Peel provides additional specials like sea bass, grouper, scallops and trout, reflecting market availability and popular demand. The seafood selection in the case is reset every day and replenished up to several times per day.

“I’m bringing everything I’ve done for the past 40 years and making it accessible…”

- Chef Mark Peel
Award winning chef + owner

St. Vincent
Meals on Wheels

Featured Charity

St. Vincent Meals on Wheels serves anyone in need that lives within our service area, regardless of age, race, illness, disability, religion or ability to pay.

All of our clients are homebound—confined to their homes because they are unable to shop or cook for themselves.

Most of our clients are seniors living below the poverty level. For these clients—who must each day choose between basic needs like shelter and medical care—our services offer a vital lifeline. In addition to seniors, we also serve disabled or chronically ill adults under age 60—such as multiple sclerosis, AIDS and diabetic patients.

For over 36 years, St. Vincent Meals on Wheels has been preparing and delivering food served with love to those who need it most. Good nutrition and daily home visits enable our clients to remain independent and in their own homes for as long as possible.

CREDIT: stvincentmealsonwheels.com
